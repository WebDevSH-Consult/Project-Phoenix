# Code of Conduct

Project Phoenix follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) in spirit:

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks will be tolerated.
- Maintainers may remove comments, commits, or contributors that violate this standard.

Reports of unacceptable behaviour can be raised directly with the maintainer.
